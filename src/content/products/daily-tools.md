## Why Another Tool Collection?

Because most existing ones are bloated with ads, require signups, or send your data to servers for no reason. Daily Tools is the antithesis: fast, private, and free forever.

## Available Tools

- **Color Picker & Converter** — HEX, RGB, HSL with live preview
- **JSON Formatter & Validator** — Pretty-print and validate JSON
- **Base64 Encoder/Decoder** — Encode and decode text and files
- **Regex Tester** — Test patterns with live highlighting
- **Lorem Ipsum Generator** — Generate placeholder text
- **Unit Converter** — Length, weight, temperature, and more
- **Text Transform** — Case conversion, character count, word count
- And many more...
