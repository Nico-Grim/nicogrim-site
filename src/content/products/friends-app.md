## The Problem

Keeping up with friend groups is scattered across five different apps. You've got a WhatsApp group for chatting, Splitwise for expenses, Google Calendar for events, and a shared album somewhere you forgot the password to.

## The Solution

Friends App unifies everything your friend group needs into a single, beautifully designed app. No more app-switching, no more "did you see my message in the other group?"

## How It Works

Create a group, invite your friends, and start using shared features immediately. Every group gets its own chat, expense tracker, event calendar, and world map. Everything stays organized per group — because your hiking crew doesn't need to see your roommates' grocery splits.
