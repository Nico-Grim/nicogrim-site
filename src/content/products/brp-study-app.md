## Built From Experience

This app was born from personal frustration with the lack of good, free study resources for the Berufsreifeprüfung. Most resources are either outdated PDFs or expensive prep courses.

## Study Smart, Not Hard

The BRP Study App uses spaced repetition to make sure you're spending time on topics that need the most work. Every session is tailored to your progress.
