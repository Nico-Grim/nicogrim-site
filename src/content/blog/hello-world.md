Welcome to my corner of the internet. I'm Nico, a developer based in Vienna, and this is where I'll share what I'm building, what I'm learning, and occasionally what I'm thinking about.

## Why a Blog?

I've been building things for a while now, but most of it has lived on my hard drive or in private repos. This site is my attempt to change that — to put my work out there and document the process.

## What to Expect

- **Project updates** — Progress on my apps, tools, and experiments
- **Technical deep dives** — Things I've learned that might save you time
- **Honest reflections** — The messy reality of building things solo

No posting schedule, no content calendar. Just writing when there's something worth sharing.

Stay tuned.
